Method 2: Shazam (Mobile App)

How It Works

  1. Download Shazam app (iOS/Android)
  2. Play the video out loud through your speakers
  3. Open Shazam and tap the button
  4. Wait for identification

PROS:

  • Works offline (can save for later identification)
  • Large music database
  • Free and widely available

CONS:

  • Requires playing audio aloud (not ideal in quiet places)
  • Phone speaker quality affects accuracy
  • Struggles with dialogue/voiceovers
  • Doesn't work well with remixes or covers
  • Can't use with saved videos viewed silently

Best for: Live situations where you can play audio aloud, or when watching videos on a different device.

Method 3: Platform's Built-in Audio Features

TikTok

Tap the spinning record icon (bottom right) to see sound details. However, this only works if the creator properly tagged the audio. Many viral sounds show "Original Sound" even when they're copyrighted music.

Instagram

Tap the audio name at the bottom of Reels. But like TikTok, this often shows "Original Audio" or the creator's username instead of the actual song name.

YouTube

Check the video description for music credits. Many YouTubers list songs, but others (especially those using royalty-free libraries like Epidemic Sound) don't credit specific tracks.

Limitation: These methods only work when creators properly tag or credit music - which often doesn't happen.

Method 4: Google Lyrics Search

How It Works

  1. Listen carefully and write down 3-5 words of lyrics you hear
  2. Google search: "[your lyrics] song lyrics"
  3. Look through results for matches

PROS:

  • Works for songs not in databases
  • Can find very obscure music
  • No tool required

CONS:

  • Only works if you can hear clear lyrics
  • Doesn't work with instrumental music
  • Time-consuming
  • Lyrics might be misheard

Best for: Songs with distinctive, clear lyrics that you can make out easily.

Method 5: Ask in Comments

How It Works

  1. Comment "song?" or "what's the song?" on the video
  2. Wait for the creator or other users to reply
  3. Check back in a few hours or days

PROS:

  • Sometimes the only option for truly custom music
  • Other users might know regional/niche songs

CONS:

  • Slow - can take hours, days, or never get answered
  • Creator may not respond
  • Other users might give wrong information
  • Have to keep checking back

Best for: Last resort when all other methods fail, especially for custom or unreleased music.

Method 6: Reverse Audio Search

For advanced users: Download the video audio, trim to the music section, and use services like AudioTag or ACRCloud's web interface.

Drawback: Requires technical knowledge, multiple steps, and isn't always more accurate than Method 1.

Troubleshooting Guide

Song Not Found?

  • Video might contain original/custom music by the creator
  • Very new release (not in databases yet)
  • Unreleased music or demo version
  • Regional music not in international databases
  • Solution: Try asking in comments or search for the creator's music credits

Wrong Song Identified?

  • Rare, but can happen with very similar-sounding songs
  • Heavy remixes might match to different versions
  • Solution: Search for the identified song + "remix" or "cover"

URL Not Working?

  • Make sure video is public (not private or deleted)
  • Copy full URL including "https://"
  • Try from official app's share button
