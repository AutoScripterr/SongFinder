How to Find a Song By Lyrics

Method 1: Google Search (Best for Lyrics)

Type the lyrics you remember in Google with quotation marks:

  • "wake me up before you go go"
  • "I see trees of green red roses too"
  • "is this the real life is this just fantasy"

Google will show the song title and artist immediately.

Method 2: Use Our Tool (If You Have a Video)

If you heard the song in a TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube video, paste the link into our identifier. We'll find the song and show you the full lyrics.

Method 3: Genius or Musixmatch

Lyric databases like Genius and Musixmatch have search functions. Type any lyrics fragment and find the song.

Tips for Better Lyric Search Results

  1. Use quotation marks - "lyrics here" tells Google to search exactly
  2. Include unique words - Distinctive lyrics work better than common phrases
  3. Try different sections - Chorus lyrics usually work best
  4. Check spelling - Misheard lyrics are common ("hold me closer Tony Danza")

Common Lyric Search Problems

"I can't understand the lyrics"

If audio is unclear, try finding the video and using our tool to identify it first.

"The lyrics are in another language"

Google Translate can help, or search for "song with lyrics" + the language.

"It's an instrumental"

No lyrics? You'll need audio recognition. Paste the video link into our tool.
